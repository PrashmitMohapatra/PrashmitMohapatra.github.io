About the Author

Fantasy • Dark Fiction • Mystery

Prashmit Mohapatra

My name is Prashmit Mohapatra, and I am a Class 7 student from Odisha, India. I enjoy reading and writing stories that explore mystery, fantasy, and darker fictional worlds.

My interest in writing grew from my love of books and the ideas they inspired. Many of my story ideas begin as small questions or random thoughts and gradually grow into larger worlds, characters, and mysteries.

I am particularly interested in stories that hide secrets beneath the surface. I enjoy creating worlds where every discovery raises new questions and where the unknown always feels just beyond reach.

My debut novel, The Map To The Forgotten World, reflects many of the themes I enjoy most: exploration, mystery, hidden truths, and the excitement of discovering something that should have remained lost.

Although this is my first published work, I hope it is only the beginning. I plan to continue writing stories that challenge imagination and invite readers into worlds unlike their own.

"Every great adventure begins with a question."
