About the Story

Some maps lead to places. This one leads to a forgotten world.

Hidden secrets, ancient mysteries, and an adventure beyond imagination await. What begins as curiosity quickly turns into a journey where every discovery raises new questions, and every path leads deeper into the unknown.

The Map To The Forgotten World is an adventure novel filled with mystery, exploration, and the thrill of uncovering what was never meant to be found.

About the Author

Prashmit Mohapatra is a young author with a passion for adventure, mystery, and imaginative storytelling. Inspired by exploration and discovery, he creates stories that transport readers into unknown worlds.

The Map To The Forgotten World is his debut novel.
