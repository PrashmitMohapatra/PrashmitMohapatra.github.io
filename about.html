About the Author

Prashmit Mohapatra

I am a young storyteller with a strong interest in fantasy, mystery, and adventure writing. I enjoy creating worlds that feel larger than life, where every detail hides something meaningful.

My writing is inspired by curiosity—the idea that there is always something hidden just beyond what we can see. I like building stories where exploration plays a key role, and where characters are forced to question what they believe is real.

Writing is not just a hobby for me; it is a way of turning imagination into something others can experience.

The Map To The Forgotten World is my debut novel and the beginning of my journey as an author.
