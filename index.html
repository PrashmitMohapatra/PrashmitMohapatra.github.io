The Story

Some maps lead to places. This one leads to a forgotten world.

Hidden secrets, ancient mysteries, and an adventure beyond imagination await. What begins as curiosity turns into a journey deeper into the unknown.

A world lost to time. A path that should not be followed. A story waiting to be discovered.

About the Author

Prashmit Mohapatra is a young author passionate about adventure, mystery, and storytelling. He enjoys creating imaginative worlds filled with discovery and wonder.

The Map To The Forgotten World is his debut novel.
