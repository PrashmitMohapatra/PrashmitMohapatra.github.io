The book

A secret below the city. A map that should never have been found.

For readers who love atmospheric quests, fog-drenched streets, forbidden knowledge, and mysteries that grow darker with every answer.

“The first rule of old places: if the silence feels alive, listen before you step forward.”

When a group of friends discovers a mysterious map hidden in the city’s abandoned infrastructure, they are thrust into a gothic dark fantasy mystery that defies explanation.

As they navigate a landscape of shadow, mist, and silence, they must outrun the chilling remnants of a lost civilization. But this is no ordinary quest — it is a race against an ancient, encroaching force that threatens to consume their reality.

As the lines between the living and the forgotten blur, the team realizes that some doors, once opened, can never be closed.

The author

Meet Prashmit Mohapatra.

Prashmit Mohapatra is a 12-year-old author and 7th grader who spent several months building the intricate, haunting world of The Map To The Forgotten World.

Inspired by dark, atmospheric storytelling, Prashmit began writing to explore how small ideas can grow into grand, imaginative journeys. His debut novel invites readers to discover what happens when they dare to look beyond the ordinary.
