About the Book

The Map To The Forgotten World is a fantasy adventure novel about discovery, mystery, and the unknown.

It follows the journey triggered by a strange and unexpected map—one that hints at a world that should not exist anymore. What lies beyond it is something far greater than ordinary imagination.

This is a story about curiosity, hidden truths, and the consequences of uncovering something that was meant to stay lost.
