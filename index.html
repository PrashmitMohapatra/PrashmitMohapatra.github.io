About Me

My name is Prashmit Mohapatra, and I am a Class 7 student from Odisha, India. I enjoy writing stories that combine fantasy, mystery, adventure, and darker fictional themes.

My interest in storytelling comes from reading books and exploring ideas that begin as simple questions. Many of those ideas eventually grow into larger worlds filled with secrets, mysteries, and hidden histories.

Apart from writing, I enjoy drawing and exploring technology. These interests often inspire new settings, characters, and ideas for future stories.

Writing & Inspiration

Fantasy, dark fiction, and mystery are the genres that interest me most. I enjoy stories that slowly reveal their secrets and encourage readers to keep asking questions.

Many of my ideas are inspired by books I read and random thoughts that appear during everyday life. A single question can sometimes become the foundation of an entire story.

I enjoy creating worlds that feel mysterious, imaginative, and filled with things waiting to be discovered.

About the Book

The Map To The Forgotten World is a fantasy adventure novel filled with mystery, exploration, and discovery.

A strange map hints at the existence of something impossible — a place that history seems to have forgotten. What follows is a journey into secrets hidden far beyond the known world.

Without revealing its surprises, the story combines adventure, mystery, and the excitement of uncovering the unknown.

Why Read This Book?

If you enjoy fantasy stories filled with secrets, forgotten places, and mysteries waiting to be uncovered, The Map To The Forgotten World offers a journey into a world where every discovery leads to something bigger.

The story is designed to create curiosity, wonder, and a sense of adventure while keeping readers guessing what lies ahead.

Future Projects

The Map To The Forgotten World is my first novel, but it is only the beginning.

I hope to continue writing fantasy and mystery stories that explore hidden worlds, forgotten histories, and adventures beyond imagination.
