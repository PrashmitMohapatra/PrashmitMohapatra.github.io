The Author

Prashmit Mohapatra

A Class 7 student from Odisha, India — and already a published novelist.

I write stories that combine fantasy, mystery, adventure, and darker fictional themes. My interest in storytelling comes from reading books and exploring ideas that begin as simple questions — questions that eventually grow into larger worlds filled with secrets and hidden histories.

Fantasy, dark fiction, and mystery are the genres that call to me most. Stories that slowly reveal their secrets. Stories that encourage readers to keep asking questions. A single question can become the foundation of an entire world.

Apart from writing, I enjoy drawing and exploring technology. These interests shape the settings, characters, and ideas that find their way into my stories.

The Map To The Forgotten World is my first novel — but only the first door into these worlds.

What's next

"I hope to continue writing fantasy and mystery stories that explore hidden worlds, forgotten histories, and adventures beyond imagination."
