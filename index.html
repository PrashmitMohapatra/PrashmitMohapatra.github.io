About Me

My name is Prashmit Mohapatra, and I am a Class 7 student from Odisha, India. I enjoy creating stories that combine fantasy, mystery, and darker fictional themes.

My interest in writing comes from my love of books and imagination. Many of my ideas begin as simple questions or random thoughts before growing into larger stories filled with hidden secrets and unexplored worlds.

Apart from writing, I enjoy drawing and exploring technology. These interests often inspire new ideas and help me imagine the worlds and characters that appear in my stories.

Writing & Inspiration

I am especially interested in fantasy, dark fiction, and mystery. I enjoy stories that slowly reveal their secrets and reward curiosity.

Many of my ideas are inspired by books I read and by unexpected thoughts that appear during everyday life. Sometimes a single question can grow into an entire world.

About the Book

The Map To The Forgotten World is a fantasy adventure novel filled with mystery, exploration, and the unknown.

A strange discovery hints at the existence of something that should no longer exist. What follows is a journey into secrets long forgotten and mysteries waiting to be uncovered.

Without revealing its surprises, the story combines adventure, discovery, hidden truths, and a growing sense of wonder.
